Property Description

Location: La Vie, Jumeirah Beach Residence, Dubai

Tereza Estates is pleased to present this Exclusive Three Bedroom apartment with Breathtaking Sea Views in La Vie, the latest ultra modern new development that is now fully completed and handed over with its very own beach club and direct sea access located on the beach of Jumeirah Beach Residence - the most popular and well established beachfront community in Dubai.

This beautiful and bright three bedroom apartment has a generous layout of 1959 sqft with floor to ceiling windows embracing the natural daylight and absolutely breathtaking uninterrupted sea views as well as Bluewaters and Dubai Eye views, The Address JBR and of course Palm Jumeirah's gem Atlantis The Royal and The Atlantis - truly the best views that Dubai beachfront properties can possibly offer now and in the future as La Vie is strategically located on the very first line of the beach itself offering its residents unbeatable advantage of what beachfront lifestyle in Dubai truly means.

This modern and bright three bedroom apartment comprises of an open plan kitchen with living and dining with seamless access to the terrace embracing the indoor outdoor seafront living feel and its stunning views of the endless Arabian sea. This gorgeous apartment has 2 terraces accessible both from living area and bedrooms.
Master bedroom suite comprises of a spacious layout with its own terrace, fitted wardrobes/dressing area in seamless white finishing and well sized ensuite bathroom with his and her's sink as well as walk in rain shower. Apart from having access to terrace windows of all bedrooms have possibility to be slightly opened so you can enjoy the fresh air without the need of air conditioning in the cooler months. Includes also maid's room and separate laundry room.

La Vie is absolutely the best option if you are looking for a beachfront lifestyle experience in the newest development thats is modern yet having that mediterranean vibe, while being in a well established community of JBR ( Jumeirah Beach Residence ), that has been one of the most popular family friendly places of Dubai to live in for over a decade. This beachfront residential community is offering variety of restaurants from casual spots for lunch by the beach, as well as fine dining options overlooking the new pride of Dubai-The Dubai Eye on Bluewaters. Shops, pharmacies, ladies and gents saloons, JBR is truly a lively community with many activities both for adults and kids.
